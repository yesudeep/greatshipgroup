Offshore and energy services. Going global.

Overview

The Greatship group has located its drilling services and offshore construction support businesses in Singapore. Singapore is presently a regional, and fast developing into a global hub for offshore oilfield services.

Bankers: ABN AMRO Bank NV, Mumbai

Greatship Global Offshore Services

Greatship Global Offshore Services Pte. Ltd (GGOS) was incorporated in 2007, with the intention of developing it into a best-in-class offshore construction services provider. The aim of this company is to operate in the more specialized domain of offshore and sub-sea construction, which requires specialized support vessels, including multi-purpose platform supply and support vessels (MPSSVs), multi-purpose support vessels (MSVs), diving support vessels and ROV support vessels.

GGOS, by 2011, will own and operate ten modern, state-of-art offshore vessels which include 2 AHTSVs, 4 MPSSVs, 2 MSVs and 2 ROVSVs to be delivered from Singapore, Colombo and India.

All our vessels are built to the highest standards of safety and operational efficiency and conform to existing and envisaged IMO and Class rules, and are capable of operating in offshore waters across the world.

The total investment commitment made in GGOS is about USD 385 million.

Greatship Global Energy Services

Greatship Global Energy Services Pte. Ltd. (GGES) was incorporated in 2006. GGES shall focus on drilling services, with the intention of becoming a globally reputed drilling contractor.

GGES made its first investment in a new-building 350-foot jack-up rig ("Greatdrill Chitra") at Keppel FELS, Singapore. Greatdrill Chitra was delivered to GGES on October 19, 2009. In addition to this owned rig, the company has also in-chartered a new-built rig of the same design ("Greatdrill Chetna") on a bare-boat basis for a period of 3 years.
