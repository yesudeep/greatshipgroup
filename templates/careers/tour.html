  • Ravi Sheth

    Ravi Sheth Managing Director

    Academic Qualifications

    • Graduated with a Bachelor of Commerce Degree from H. R. College, Mumbai in 1982.
    • Graduated with a Master of Business Administration from Babson College, Wellesley, U.S.A. in 1985.

    Work Experience and Profile

    Co-founded Godrej Foods Limited (a joint venture between The Great Eastern Shipping Co. Ltd. and Godrej Soaps Ltd.) and worked as Vice-President (Finance) from 1985 to 1989.

    Joined The Great Eastern Shipping Co. Ltd. on July 1, 1990 and is currently its Executive Director. The Great Eastern Shipping Co. Ltd. is India's largest private sector shipping company, owning and operating a fleet comprising crude and product tankers, gas carrier and bulk carriers. For the Financial Year ended March 31, 2008, The Great Eastern Shipping Co. Ltd. recorded revenue of Rs.3204 crores and net profits of Rs. 1357 crores. It was rated as Asia's 3rd most profitable company by Forbes Asia, 200, best under a billion.

    Presently, the Managing Director of Greatship (India) Limited, a wholly owned subsidiary of The Great Eastern Shipping Co. Ltd., with a projected capital outlay in excess of US$ 900 million.

    He is a strategic investor in companies with good governance and robust revenue model. He has been instrumental in identifying potential growth stories with sound business fundamentals.
